### üìã Functional Requirements

- [ ] The instructor shall use a **mobile-compatible scanner interface**.
- [ ] The system shall allow scanning of exam papers using **existing image recognition technology**.
- [ ] The interface shall provide real-time feedback on scan quality.
- [ ] The scanned images shall be securely uploaded for processing.
- [ ] The system shall support multiple paper formats and layouts.

### üß© Use Case Scenario

**Actor:** Instructor  
**Precondition:** Instructor has access to a mobile device with the scanner interface.

**Steps:**
1. Instructor opens the mobile-compatible scanner interface.
2. Instructor selects the option to scan exam papers.
3. Instructor aligns the exam paper within the camera frame.
4. The system uses image recognition technology to capture and process the image.
5. The interface provides real-time feedback on scan quality.
6. Instructor reviews the scanned image and confirms upload.
7. The scanned paper is securely uploaded for result processing.

**Postcondition:** Exam paper is successfully scanned and uploaded for further processing.
